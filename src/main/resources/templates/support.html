How can we help?

FAQ

How can I book a flight with your company?

To book a flight, visit our website and use our user-friendly booking tool. Simply enter your departure and destination cities, travel dates, and passenger information. Follow the prompts to complete your booking.

What payment methods do you accept for flight bookings?

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other popular online payment options. You can choose your preferred payment method during the booking process.

Can I make changes to my flight booking after it's been confirmed?

Yes, you can make changes to your booking, such as flight date or passenger information, depending on the fare rules and availability. Visit our website or contact our customer support for assistance with modifications.

What is your baggage policy and fees?

Our baggage policy and fees vary depending on the type of ticket you've purchased. You can find detailed information on our website, including weight and size limits, as well as any applicable fees.

How can I check in for my flight?

You can check in at the airport using our self-service kiosks or by visiting the check-in counter.

What is your policy on flight delays and cancellations?

We understand that delays and cancellations can be inconvenient. We strive to minimize disruptions, but in case of such events, we'll notify you as soon as possible and provide rebooking options or compensation, in line with our policies and regulations.

How do I request special assistance for passengers with disabilities or medical conditions?

We offer special assistance for passengers with disabilities or medical conditions. Please notify us of your specific needs during the booking process or contact our customer support to arrange for assistance.

Can I request a refund for my ticket, and what is the refund process?

Refund eligibility and processes may vary based on your ticket type. To request a refund, visit our website and follow the refund request instructions or contact our customer support for guidance.

Do you offer in-flight services like meals and entertainment?

Yes, we provide in-flight services, including meal options and entertainment, depending on the flight's duration and ticket class. You can find details about these services on our website.

How can I contact customer support for further assistance?

Our customer support team is available 24/7 to assist you. You can reach us via email, or live chat on our website. Visit the "Contact Us" page for all the contact options.
